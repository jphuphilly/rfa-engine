# Humane AI Pin — Product Spec (Retroactive, as of late 2023)

## Company
Humane Inc., founded by ex-Apple employees Imran Chaudhri and Bethany Bongiorno.
Raised $230M+ in funding from investors including Qualcomm, Microsoft, Tiger Global,
and Sam Altman. Valued at approximately $850M pre-launch.

## The Product
The Humane AI Pin is a wearable AI device that clips to your clothing. It has no
screen. It uses a laser projector to display information on your palm, a camera for
visual context, a microphone/speaker for voice interaction, and its own cellular
connection (via T-Mobile). It runs a custom operating system called Cosmos OS powered
by a large language model.

## Price Point
$699 device + $24/month subscription for cellular connectivity, cloud AI processing,
and a dedicated phone number.

## Core Features
- Voice-first AI assistant (ask questions, get answers, no screen required)
- Laser Ink Display projects text and simple UI onto your palm
- AI-powered camera that can identify objects, translate text, and provide nutritional
  information by looking at food
- Phone calls and messaging via dedicated number
- Music playback (Tidal integration)
- "Catch Me Up" feature that summarizes notifications and messages
- Real-time language translation
- No wake word required — activated by touch or gesture

## Target User
Tech-forward early adopters who want to reduce screen time and smartphone dependency.
People who believe the smartphone era is ending and want to be first to the next
computing paradigm.

## The Pitch
"The smartphone was a revolutionary device, but it has become an addiction machine.
The AI Pin is the first device designed for the AI era — screenless, seamless, and
ambient. It lets you stay connected without being tethered to a screen. It's not a
phone replacement; it's a phone evolution."

## Technology
- Qualcomm Snapdragon processor
- Custom Cosmos OS
- Proprietary laser projection system
- Multiple cameras and sensors
- T-Mobile cellular connectivity
- Cloud-based AI processing (partnerships with OpenAI and Microsoft)
